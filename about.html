About Florida for All

Florida for All is a grassroots movement committed to electing a new generation of diverse, progressive, working-class leaders. Founded in 2025, our mission is to transform Florida's economy and democracy by prioritizing the needs of everyday Floridians over corporate interests. As of 09:35 PM EDT on Saturday, July 19, 2025, our team is actively engaging communities across the state. Learn how you can get involved today!
