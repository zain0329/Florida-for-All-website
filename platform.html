Our Platform

Florida for All is committed to a platform that prioritizes affordable healthcare, renewable energy, education reform, and economic justice. We advocate for policies that benefit all Floridians, not just the wealthy elite. Our vision includes:

  • Universal access to quality healthcare.
  • Transition to 100% clean energy by 2035.
  • Free community college and trade school education.
  • Fair wages and worker protections.
