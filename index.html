ABOUT FLORIDA FOR ALL

Florida for All is a grassroots movement committed to electing a new generation of diverse, progressive, working-class leaders. Founded in 2025, our mission is to transform Florida's economy and democracy by prioritizing the needs of everyday Floridians over corporate interests. Learn how you can get involved today!

NOMINATE A CANDIDATE

Do you know a passionate individual ready to fight for Florida's future? Nominate them to join our movement. Fill out our nomination form to start the process and help us build a stronger, more representative leadership.

MEET OUR CANDIDATES

Our candidates are dedicated community leaders from across Florida, bringing fresh perspectives to the table. Stay tuned for profiles and updates as we announce our 2025 election slate.

OUR PLATFORM

Our platform focuses on affordable healthcare, renewable energy, education reform, and economic justice. We advocate for policies that benefit all Floridians, not just the wealthy elite. Explore our detailed policy proposals and join the conversation.
